Clear Browser Cache Instructions

Your browser is loading a cached build. Follow these steps:

  1. Open DevTools (F12 or Cmd+Option+I)
  2. Go to Application tab (Chrome) or Storage tab (Firefox)
  3. Click "Clear site data" or "Clear storage"
  4. Check all boxes and click "Clear"
  5. Close all tabs with localhost:5173
  6. Open a new tab and go to http://localhost:5173
  7. Hard refresh: Cmd+Shift+R (Mac) or Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows)

OR use Incognito/Private window:

  1. Open a new Incognito/Private window
  2. Go to http://localhost:5173
  3. This bypasses all cache
